Simple Interest Calculator

The formula for accruing interest on a principa amount of money is
  A  = P(1 + rt)   where:

A = Total money accrued (principal + interest)
P = Principal amount of investment
r = rate of interest per year
t = time (number of years)

Input values for Principal (P), interest rate (r) and time (t in years)  to calculate total investment after 't' years
